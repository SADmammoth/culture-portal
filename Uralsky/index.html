Photographers of Belarus

Uralsky, Ivan

1982 - present

Ivan Uralsky is a modern street photographer. Over the years of “presence” in the genre, he passed from photos of water droplets to a personal exhibition of street photos. His oeuvre calls to stop, to hear your breath and come closer to life.
